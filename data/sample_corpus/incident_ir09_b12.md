**Asset:** B-12
**Severity:** medium

**Title:** Pressure excursion near-miss on Boiler B-12

Amit Sharma flagged this as a reason to prioritize closing the outstanding relief valve test evidence gap (see WO-1102 and the Q4 inspection report) given REG-045 emergency shutdown testing expectations.
