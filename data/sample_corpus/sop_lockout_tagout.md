# SOP: Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)

Applies to all rotating equipment (pumps, compressors) and pressurized equipment (boilers, relief valves) prior to maintenance, per OSHA-105.

## Steps
1. Notify affected personnel and the Shift Supervisor (e.g. Maria Chen).
2. Identify all energy sources (electrical, pressure, stored mechanical energy) for the asset.
3. Shut down equipment using normal stopping procedure.
4. Isolate energy sources and apply locks/tags.
5. Verify zero energy state before beginning work (e.g. bleed residual pressure on pump casings or relief valve piping).
6. On completion, remove locks/tags in reverse order and confirm with the Safety Officer (Priya Nair) before returning equipment to service.

PPE requirements per REG-031 (PPE Compliance Directive) apply throughout.
