# SOP: Centrifugal Pump Mechanical Seal Replacement

Applies to: Centrifugal Pumps (e.g. P-101, P-102).

## 1. Safety preparation
Isolate and lock out the pump per OSHA-105 (Lockout/Tagout Standard) before beginning work. Priya Nair (Safety Officer) must sign off on isolation for any pump with a criticality of high or above.

## 2. Procedure
1. Drain and depressurize the casing.
2. Remove coupling guard and disconnect coupling.
3. Withdraw seal assembly; inspect seal faces for scoring, chipping, or uneven wear (a common early indicator of misalignment or cavitation - see Pump P-101's repeated failure history under WO-1041, WO-1052, and WO-1067 for a worked example of what NOT catching this looks like).
4. Check shaft runout and alignment before installing the new seal.
5. Install OEM-spec replacement seal (or approved cartridge-style upgrade, see WO-1089).
6. Reassemble, re-align, and perform a controlled startup with vibration monitoring.

## 3. Reporting
Log the work order under the affected asset tag and report per REG-022 (Rotating Equipment Seal Integrity Standard). Repeat failures within 6 months on the same asset must be escalated to the Maintenance Lead (Sam Reyes) for root-cause investigation rather than closed as routine repairs.
