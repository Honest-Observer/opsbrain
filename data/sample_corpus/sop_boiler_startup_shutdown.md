# SOP: Package Boiler Startup and Shutdown

Applies to: Package Boilers (e.g. B-12).

## 1. Pre-startup checks
Confirm low-water cutoff test current, burner management system test current, and relief valve (e.g. V-045) bench-test certificate on file and within the frequency required by REG-052 (Relief Valve Testing Frequency) and REG-014 (Boiler Pressure Vessel Inspection Code). Do not start the boiler if the relief valve test evidence is missing or expired - escalate to the Compliance Auditor (Linda Osei) instead.

## 2. Startup sequence
1. Confirm feedwater supply and chemistry within spec.
2. Purge combustion chamber per burner manufacturer guidance.
3. Light burner at low fire, ramp gradually while monitoring drum pressure.
4. Confirm safety interlocks and emergency shutdown (ESD) function per REG-045 before ramping to full load.

## 3. Shutdown sequence
1. Reduce load gradually.
2. Secure fuel supply and confirm burner off.
3. Allow natural cooldown before isolating for maintenance access.
