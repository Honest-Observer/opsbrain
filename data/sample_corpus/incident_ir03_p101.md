# Incident Report IR-03

**Asset:** P-101
**Severity:** low
**Date:** 2025-04-02
**Title:** Near-miss: seal weep observed on Pump P-101

During a routine shift round, Shift Supervisor Maria Chen observed a small amount of weeping at the P-101 mechanical seal, well before any alarm threshold. No injury, no environmental release. Logged as a near-miss and flagged for maintenance follow-up. In hindsight this was an early warning sign of the seal failure pattern that recurred in WO-1041 and WO-1052, and culminated in the more serious Incident IR-07 four months later.
